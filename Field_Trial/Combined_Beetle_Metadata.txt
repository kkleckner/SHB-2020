This is the metadata for Combined_Beetle_Data.csv
The data set and all other files can be found at https://github.com/kkleckner/SHB-2020

A single row represents 1 hive with 2 boxes (each with a trap) in the pilot study.


For each column:

Header | Description

Treatment | Compound mixed into pollen and adminsitered to hives
            Acetone - Negative control; Acetamiprid; Fipronil - Positive control

Live_Dead | Type of beetles collected from hive after two week treatment period
            Live - live beetles aspirated from hive
            Dead - dead beetles collected from inside traps

Count | Number of beetles (either live or dead) collected after two week treament period
