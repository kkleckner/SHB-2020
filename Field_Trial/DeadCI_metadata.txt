This is the metadata for DeadCI.csv
The data set and all other files can be found at https://github.com/kkleckner/SHB-2020

For each column:

Header | Description

Treatment | Compound mixed into pollen and adminsitered to hives in CD traps
            Acetone - Negative control; Acetamiprid; Fipronil - Positive control

DeadMean | Average number of dead beetles collected from inside traps for 5 hives in treatment group
           Beetles collected after two week treatment period

Lower | Lower bound of 95% confidence interval 
        Calculated by emmeans and with negative binomial distribution

Upper | Upper bound of 95% confidence interval
        Calculated by emmeans and with negative binomial distribution
